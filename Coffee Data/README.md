# Coffee Chain Dataset – Tableau Project
### Submitted By: Abishek A (ENG23AM0100)

This folder contains Tableau visualizations based on the Coffee Chain dataset.  
A total of 20 tasks were completed using heatmaps, bar charts, scatter plots, boxplots, bubble charts, treemaps, and line charts.

## Tasks Summary
1. Heatmap – Lowest profit state for Espresso.
2. Boxplot – Espresso product with highest sales distribution.
3. Bar chart – Profit comparison by product.
4. Scatter plot – Sales vs Profit across states.
5. Identify highest & lowest profit states.
6. Sales of Decaf Espresso in Nevada.
7. Tea contribution to overall profit (%).
8. Average marketing for area codes 660 & 818.
9. Highest & lowest profit products in California.
10. Minimum marketing for Coffee Beans Colombian.
11–12. Decaf sales contribution in East market (overall & 2012).
13. Average profit for products starting with ‘C’.
14. Top 5 products by sales in Central region.
15. 2013 highest profit state in West market.
16. Expenses-to-sales ratio for lowest profit state.
17. Distinct area codes for lowest budget margin (small market).
18. % of total profit for Caffe Mocha in Major Market (2013).
19. Month/year when Decaf sales crossed $1100 (Colorado & Florida).
20. Treemap – Market with maximum budget sales.
